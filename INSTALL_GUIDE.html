Host Online for Free (GitHub Pages)

To install on your phone, the app needs to be online with HTTPS. GitHub Pages is completely free and takes about 10 minutes.

1. Create a free account at github.com
2. Click the "+" button ‚Üí "New repository"
3. Name it forge-gym, select Public, click Create repository
4. Click "uploading an existing file" and drag in ALL files from your gym app folder
5. Click "Commit changes"
6. Go to Settings ‚Üí Pages, under Source select "Deploy from branch", branch: main, folder: / (root), click Save
7. Wait 2‚Äì3 minutes. Your app is now live at: https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/forge-gym/

Free & permanent: GitHub Pages is free forever for public repositories. Your data stays on your device (localStorage), not on GitHub.

Get a Real Android APK (Optional)

If you want a proper .apk file that can be shared and sideloaded on Android:

1. Complete Step 3 first (your app must be hosted online)
2. Go to pwabuilder.com
3. Paste your GitHub Pages URL and click "Start"
4. Click "Package for Stores" ‚Üí "Android"
5. Click "Generate" ‚Äî it downloads a ZIP with your .apk file
6. On your Android phone, enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in Settings
7. Transfer the APK to your phone and tap it to install

PWABuilder is free and made by Microsoft. The APK it generates uses Chrome's TWA (Trusted Web Activity) engine ‚Äî high quality and approved for Google Play Store submission.
